Sir Sidney Poitier

Trailblazer for Black Actors

Here's a time line of Sir Poitier's Life:

  • 1927 - Born in Miami, Florida
  • 1950 - Stars in First Film "No Way Out"
  • 1958 - First Golden Globe Nomination for "Edge of the City"
  • 1963 - Won the "Best Actor" Academy Award for "Lillies of the Field"
  • 1974 - Knighted
  • 1997 - Became the Bahamian Ambassador to Japan
  • 2000 - Receives a Grammy for "Best Spoken Word Album"- "The Measure of a Man"
  • 2009 - Receives a Medal of Freedom from President Barack Obama
  • 2022 - dies at the age of 94.

"To simply wake up every morning a better person than when I went to bed."

--Sir Sidney Poitier
